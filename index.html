A Game by Tom Hsu

Controls

W = Jump,  S = Down,   A = Left,   D = Right,   Mouse = shoot

Instructions

In a science experiment gone wrong, Mario has turned into an evil character and is trying to kill you. Do whatever it takes to stay alive and kill him. Avoid running into Mario and dodge his red bullets 3
times or else you will die. Hit Mario with your green bullets 10 times to win!
